>Spring 2020

Music Festivals Impact on DJ Popularity

It makes sense that up-and-coming DJs make their careers at music festivals with their ever increasing size and popularity. To see if this is true, we try to quantify the effect of these performances on their popularity.

MLB Hit Predictor

This quarter, our team has been diving deep into a Major League Baseball Data Science project, where our primary goal is to predict which players are going to get a 'base hit' on any given day. We explore interesting data sets that have to do with baseball matchups, weather data, and ballpark dimensions. Our inspiration for this project comes from an app called 'Beat the Streak', where anybody can win 5.6 million dollars if you correctly pick players to get hits 57 times in a row. Don't worry, we will be sure to split the proceeds with the rest of DSU.
