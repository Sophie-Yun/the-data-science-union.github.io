Our Projects


> Fall 2021

Predicting NBA Players' Salaries

An NBA player’s annual salary is determined by the player’s impact which is measured by various factors. Our project aimed to develop a machine learning model that would predict an NBA player’s annual salary based on the player’s game statistics.

Building an Age Guesser using Transfer Learning

The Internet Movie Database (IMDB) keeps records of 500,000+ celebrities, including their headshots and ages. Our project applies transfer learning to this database in order to build a program that, given a person's photo, accurately predicts their age.

> Spring 2020

Music Festivals Impact on DJ Popularity

It makes sense that up-and-coming DJs make their careers at music festivals with their ever increasing size and popularity. To see if this is true, we try to quantify the effect of these performances on their popularity.

MLB Hit Predictor

Our primary goal is to predict which players are going to get a 'base hit' on any given day. Our inspiration for this project comes from an app called 'Beat the Streak', where anybody can win 5.6 million dollars if you correctly pick players to get hits 57 times in a row.
