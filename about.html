{About Us}

Cultivating a thriving community of data science professionals at UCLA

Curriculum {

Our club's curriculum supplements and enhances UCLA's statistics coursework by putting the knowledge together in a format useful for data science. We implement theoretical statistics concepts through Python and SQL.

Projects {

Projects are an opportunity for members to work collaboratively, investigate meaningful questions, and draw insightful conclusions. Members will gain practical knowledge in applying the data science process, hands-on experience with working on a team, and get to know people in the club better. After participating in an internal project, members get to branch out and work with our professional partners on client projects.

Professional Development {

Since our ultimate goal is to serve as a pipeline towards careers in data science, we offer opportunities such as: networking events with working professionals, industry panels with data science experts, career strategy workshops focusing on the job search process, and professional connections with our alumni and client network.
